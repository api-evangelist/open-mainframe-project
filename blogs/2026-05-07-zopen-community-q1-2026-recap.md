Author: Haritha Ananthakrishnan – Advisory Research Software Engineer at IBM and Open Mainframe Project Ambassador The zopen community had a strong first quarter. Here’s a look at what the community shipped, fixed, and has coming next. What Is the zopen Community? The zopen community is a global group of z/OS enthusiasts dedicated to making popular open source tools available on z/OS. Newly contributed to the Open Mainframe Project, the community now hosts more than 200 open source projects spanning build tools, editors, networking, security, AI, and more.
