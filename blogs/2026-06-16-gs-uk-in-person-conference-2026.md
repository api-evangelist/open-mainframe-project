The annual GS UK conference will be held November 2-5, 2026, at Whittlebury Hall in Northamptonshire. The call for papers opens June 1 with a July 31 submission deadline, welcoming presentations on Open Mainframe Project initiatives and modernization topics under a theme of collaboration, learning, and advancing the future of enterprise computing.
